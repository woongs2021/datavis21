Data Visualization 2021S

Welcome, class!

As the volume and complexity of data increases, the importance of visually representing such data is also increasing. Data visualization plays an important role in bringing meaning to complex data and is used to help solve complex problems. Students learn key design principles and get hands-on visualization experiences using tools such as Tableau, Python, and/or D3.js (javascript library). Students work through a visualization project in individuals or teams to experience processes and methodologies for identifying problems and deriving solutions.

This course is offered as a Blending lecture, which consists of video lectures (for take-home) and real-time online lectures via Zoom. Students are required to watch the video lecture and complete/submit the given assignment or readings before the following week’s lecture begins.
